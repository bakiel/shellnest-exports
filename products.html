Western Cape Origin

Rooibos Tea

Harvested from the Cederberg mountains of the Western Cape, our rooibos tea is one of South Africa's most celebrated exports. This remarkable plant grows nowhere else on Earth, making it a truly unique offering for international markets.

Naturally caffeine-free and rich in antioxidants, rooibos has gained worldwide recognition as a premium health beverage. Our rooibos is harvested, fermented, and dried using traditional methods that preserve its distinctive earthy flavour and deep amber colour.

Origin: Cederberg region, Western Cape, South Africa. The only place on Earth where Aspalathus linearis grows naturally.

Certifications: HACCP, FSSC 22000, EU Organic, USDA Organic, Rainforest Alliance (select farms).

Available formats: Bulk loose-leaf, cut tea (various grades), tea bags, fine-cut for sachets, extract powder, and custom blends. Private labelling available for wholesale buyers.

Key markets: EU, UK, USA, Japan, Middle East, South Korea

Fynbos Region

Honeybush Tea

Honeybush (Cyclopia) is a naturally sweet herbal tea indigenous to the fynbos biome of the Western and Eastern Cape. Its distinctive honey-like aroma and sweet flavour make it a rising star in global health-tea markets.

Low in tannins and caffeine-free, honeybush is rich in polyphenols and antioxidants. It has been used in South African traditional medicine for centuries and is now gaining scientific recognition for its health benefits.

Origin: Fynbos biome, Western and Eastern Cape. Part of the Cape Floristic Region, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and global biodiversity hotspot.

Certifications: HACCP, FSSC 22000, EU Organic (select batches), wild-harvested sustainability certification.

Available formats: Loose-leaf, cut tea, extract powder, and organic-certified options. Custom blending with rooibos or other botanicals available.

Key markets: EU (growing demand), UK, USA, South Korea, Japan

Mpumalanga Origin

Macadamia Nuts

South Africa is the world's largest producer of macadamia nuts, and our premium-grade product comes from the subtropical orchards of Mpumalanga and Limpopo. Rich, buttery, and versatile, macadamia nuts are one of the most sought-after tree nuts globally.

Our macadamias are harvested at peak maturity, processed in HACCP-certified facilities, and graded according to strict international standards. We supply both raw (in-shell and kernel) and processed varieties.

Origin: Subtropical orchards of Mpumalanga and Limpopo provinces. South Africa produces over 50,000 tonnes annually, making it the global leader.

Certifications: HACCP, FSSC 22000, BRC Global Standard, kosher and halal certification available on request.

Available formats: In-shell, whole kernel (Style 0-4), halves, pieces, macadamia oil, roasted, salted, and chocolate-coated. Private labelling and custom processing available.

Key markets: China, USA, EU, Japan, Middle East, India, South Korea
