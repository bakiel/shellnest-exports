Our streamlined export process ensures your products arrive on time, in perfect condition, and fully compliant with international trade regulations.

How We Work

Our 5-Step Process

We handle every aspect of the export journey, from sourcing and quality control to documentation and logistics.

01

Sourcing & Selection

We partner directly with verified farms across South Africa's key agricultural regions. Each farm is assessed for quality consistency, sustainability practices, and fair labour standards. Only products that meet our premium criteria are selected for export.

02

Quality Assurance & Testing

Every batch undergoes laboratory testing for purity, freshness, moisture content, and microbiological safety. We test against the specific regulations of each destination country, including EU, US FDA, and Asian market standards.

03

Processing & Packaging

Products are processed and packaged in HACCP and FSSC 22000 certified facilities. We offer standard bulk packaging, retail-ready packaging, and full private labelling services for brands looking to enter the South African product market.

04

Documentation & Compliance

We handle all export documentation: phytosanitary certificates, certificates of origin, commercial invoices, packing lists, bills of lading, and customs declarations. Every shipment is fully compliant before it leaves South Africa.

05

Shipping & Delivery

We coordinate logistics through the ports of Durban and Cape Town with trusted international freight partners. We offer both sea cargo (FCL and LCL) and air freight options, with real-time tracking from origin to destination.

Markets We Serve

Global Reach

European Union

Our largest export market. Full EU compliance including Novel Food regulations for honeybush, organic certification, and EU labelling standards. We navigate the complexities of TRACES NT and EU phytosanitary requirements.

United Kingdom

Post-Brexit market with specific import requirements. We navigate UK DEFRA regulations, health certificates, and labelling compliance. Our established UK buyer network streamlines the import process.

United States

FDA-compliant exports with Prior Notice filing, FSVP compliance, and US labelling requirements. We leverage AGOA trade agreement benefits for preferential duty rates on qualifying products.

Asia & Middle East

Growing markets for macadamia nuts (China, Japan) and teas (South Korea, UAE). We handle halal certification, Mandarin and Arabic labelling, and Asian market-specific packaging requirements.

What Buyers Receive

Full Export Documentation

Every ShellNest shipment arrives with a complete documentation package that ensures smooth customs clearance and regulatory compliance in your market.

Certificates -- Phytosanitary Certificate (DALRRD issued), Certificate of Origin, HACCP Compliance Certificate, and organic certification where applicable.

Commercial Documents -- Commercial Invoice, Packing List, Bill of Lading or Air Waybill, and Insurance Certificate for every consignment.

Quality Reports -- Laboratory analysis reports, moisture content verification, microbiological test results, and product specification sheets per batch.
